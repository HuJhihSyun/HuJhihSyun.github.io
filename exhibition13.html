    Intelligent Access Platform 5G Anyhaul & Extra Broadband

    Intelligent Access Platform 5G Anyhaul & Extra Broadband

    Nokia future proof broadband intelligent access platform is designed and implemented with ”faster, better and smarter” in mind. Nokia solution allows operators to use it as a unified platform to faster multiple service offerings including HSI, triple play, business, network slicing and mobile any-haul, to ensure better end user broadband experiences at home and at work; and be smarter to evolve meeting diversified and ever demanding network performances of future 5G eMBB, mMTC and URLLC. You can’t see the future, but you can get ready for it, and your investment of today can be safely protected.

    Increase revenues and connect new customers with 5G Fixed Wireless Access

    With 5G, lightning-fast speeds can be enjoyed anywhere, so why not enjoy it in your Livingroom. After all, 5G has the horsepower to compete with traditional fixed access technologies to provide a superior service at gigabit speeds.In this traditional demo, we will show Nokia’s FastMile 5G gateway.

    • Static gateway for customers to hold and discover
    • Show the ultra-reliability across the Intelligent Mesh network

    Extend your fiber network with wireless drops

    Nokia is the world leader in ultra-broadband access solutions. Our technology powers the fastest, largest and most advanced networks in the world. With our new Wireless PON solution, you can take your ultra-broadband services further than ever before.

    諾基亞面對未來的寬頻智慧接取網路著重於設計和整合，落實在「更快」、「更好」、「更智慧」的接取網路平台基礎上。諾基亞方案允許電信業者將其作為一個單一整合平台，以更快速地提供多重服務，包括 HSI、三重播放 (triple play)、商務企業用戶、網路切片技術和未來 5G 訊號回傳 (Any-haul)，以確保使用者在居家和工作環境中提供更好的用戶頻寬體驗；更能簡潔、精巧的從事多種商務經營模式，滿足未來 5G eMBB，mMTC 和 URLLC 的多樣化和苛刻的網路性能。你無法看到未來，但你可以做好準備，為今日的投資選擇最佳保障。

    諾基亞 5G 讓使用者即使在客廳仍可以享受光速般的網路，和寬頻網路一樣可提供 gigabit 的速度。
    在這裡我們將展示 5G Gateway 和 Intelligent Mesh 所帶來更佳的網路覆蓋。

    諾基亞是寬頻技術的領導者，我們的技術涵蓋了最快且先進的網路。有了 Wireless PON 解決方案，運營商可以延伸寬頻的服務到更多的地方。
