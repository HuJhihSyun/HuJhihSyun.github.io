Exhibition / 應用展示

Operational efficiency with private LTE

Showcase the scope and value of Private LTE (enterprise direct or via CSP) that help verticals (airports/mining) digitalize their operation for intelligent insights and enhanced productivity.

Technical showcases include pLTE reliability enhancement possibilities (Dual Modem IP connectivity, and Dual Connectivity R15), a live demo of LTE industrial ecosystem (Worker fatigue/health monitoring, machine connectivity), and indoor geo-tracking.

AR use case demonstrations of pLTE include use at airport gates to improve plane turn around speed and at open pit mines to help improve safety and automation.

展示 Private LTE（企業直接或透過電信業者）的範圍和價值，pLTE 幫助垂直產業（機場 / 採礦業）營運數位化，協助產業發展智慧洞察分析能力及生產力的提升。

技術展示包括 Plte 增強可靠度的可能性(透過雙 IP 連接和 Release 15 中的雙連接）， LTE 產業生態系統的現場展示（工作者疲勞 / 健康監測，機器連接）以及室內定位跟踪。

AR 應用案例將展示 pLTE 應用於機場機門使用以提高機務流暢度以及在露天礦場中，提高安全性和自動化。
