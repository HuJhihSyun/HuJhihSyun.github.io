REGISTRATION

Ragheed Al-dabbagh

Head of Optical Networks Product Management, Architecture & Consulting Engineering Nokia - APJ, India, Greater China

Ragheed 是諾基亞亞太地區光纖網路產品管理、架構和顧問部門負責人。他曾在諾基亞 IP 和光纖網路部門領導大洋洲的光纖業務開發，擔任過各種技術工程職務。他在電信業擁有超過 10 年的經驗，尤其擅長傳輸網路領域（xWDM、OTN、Packet、T-SDN）。

加入諾基亞之前，Ragheed 曾在 Pacific Broadband Networks （PBN）擔任 xPON 研發工程師，之後加入 Vicom Australia 擔任光纖測試和測量專家。

出生於伊拉克巴格達，在澳洲接受教育，Ragheed 在澳洲斯威本科技大學取得電信工程、資訊工程和軟體工程雙學位，以優異成績畢業，專攻纖維光學和光纖傳輸系統。目前居住在中國上海。

Ragheed is the Nokia Asia Pacific head of Optical Networks Product Management, Architecture, and Consulting. Having previously led Optical business development for Oceania, in the IP & Optical Networks Division at Nokia as well as various other technical engineering roles. He has over 10 years experience in the Telecommunications industry with particular expertise in the area of Transport Networks (xWDM, OTN, Packet, T-SDN).

Prior to joining Nokia, Ragheed worked for Pacific Broadband Networks (PBN) as an xPON R&D engineer before joining Vicom Australia as an Optical test & measurement specialist.

Born in Baghdad, Iraq and educated in Australia. Ragheed received his undergraduate double degree in Telecommunications Engineering, Computer Science and Software Engineering from Swinburne University of Technology, where he graduated with honors and specialized in Photonics and Fibre Optic Transmission Systems. He currently resides in Shanghai, China.
