    REGISTRATION

    Kent Wong

    Vice President and Head of IP/Optical Networks Regional Business Center Asia Pacific, Nokia Networks

    Vice president and head of IP/Optical Networks Regional Business Center Asia Pacific (ION RBC APAC), Nokia

    Drawing on 20 years of experience in the field of telecommunications and data networking, Kent Wong currently leads the IP/Optical Networks Regional Business Center Asia Pacific of Nokia. In this role, Kent is responsible for end-to-end Nokia IP and Optical business in Asia Pacific, including business development, solution positioning, product management, consulting engineering, go-to-market and commercial strategy, as well as customer engineering and delivery.

    Kent joined Nokia through the acquisition of Alcatel-Lucent, where he led the IP and Optical business for Asia Pacific since 2013. Before that, Kent also served in several leader’s roles for IP business and solutions within Alcatel-Lucent Asia Pacific region, including the head of IP Competence Center, IP business development for North Asia, and senior IP product manager for Asia Pacific.

    Prior to Alcatel-Lucent, Kent held position in Research & Development (R&D) for the IP Service Router in TiMetra Networks, which was acquired by Alcatel in 2003. Kent also worked in various R&D roles for IP products at Nortel Networks before he joined TiMetra Networks.

    Kent has extensive knowledge and rich experience in IP/MPLS and Optical networking in both the business and technology aspects. He holds a BS in Electrical Engineering from Cornell University and a MS in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University.

    擁有 20 年在電信及數據網路的產業經驗，Kent Wong 目前負責諾基亞亞太區區域業務中心 IP 與光纖網路部門，拓展亞太區諾基亞 IP 與光纖端到端業務，包括業務開發、解決方案擬定、產品管理、工程諮詢、產品商轉策略、以及客戶工程和交付。

    Kent 自 2013 年起即負責亞太區 IP 與光纖業務，因諾基亞與阿爾卡特-朗訊合併而加入諾基亞。之前則擔任阿爾卡特-朗訊亞太區 IP 業務和解決方案相關業務主管職，包括 IP 能力發展中心負責人、北亞 IP 業務開發、及亞太區 IP 資深產品經理。

    加入阿爾卡特-朗訊之前，Kent 服務於 TiMetra Networks 擔任 IP 服務路由器研發人員，該公司於 2003 年被阿爾卡特併購。更早之前，Kent 則於北電網絡公司從事 IP 產品的各種研發職務。

    Kent 在 IP/MPLS 和光纖網路的商業與技術領域，均具備廣泛的知識和豐富的經驗。他擁有康乃爾大學電機工程學士學位及史丹佛大學電機工程碩士學位。
