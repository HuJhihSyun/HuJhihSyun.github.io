Exhibition / 應用展示

5G-ready Optical Networks

Comprehensive Fronthaul solutions
The evolution of the fronthaul solutions moving from P2P fiber approaches that use an unmanaged, dedicated fiber per CPRI stream to those leverage WDM for fiber efficiency by creating channels within the fiber. The Active Transparent solution introduces wavelength translation capability that avoid having to touch or upgrade the RAN equipment and or RAN processes. 25Gbps interfaces to support 5G radio
With the coming of 5G and the need to support higher rates, Nokia 1830 VWM portfolio will evolve to support a new TLU-200 having support for 10Gbps and 25Gbps interfaces. The TLU-200 provides the colorization of B/W interfaces so the radios can remain untouched by continuing to use B/W optics.

全面的 Front-haul 解決方案
Front-haul 解決方案的演進，由單純的點對點使用實體專用光纖方式，該專用光纖無法被管理，同時也只供單一 CRPI 訊流使用，逐漸演進至在實體光纖中導入 WDM 技術，創造出眾多通道在單一實體光纖內，進而提供效率。而主動透通式的解決方案，導入了波長轉換的能力，提供了另一種選擇可以避免在既有的 RAN 設備上做任何異動，卻同時可以使用 WDM Fronthaul 的光纖效率。 25Gbps 介面以支援 5G Radio
迎接 5G 世代的來臨與更高速率的需求，Nokia 1830 VWM 系列產品也對應推出 TLU-200 型號，可以同時支援 10Gbps 與 25Gbps 的介面。TLU-200 具備波長轉換的功能，能在無需更動 RAN 設備既有組態的情況下，提供 25Gbps WDM 的 Fronthaul 解決方案。
