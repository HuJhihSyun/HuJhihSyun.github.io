REGISTRATION

Markus Borchert

President of Nokia Greater China

馬博策 (Markus Borchert) 先生在通信、IT 和 IT 服務產業擁有超過 25 年的國際工作經驗，是一位擅長引領跨國公司實現轉型的領導者。

自 2019 年 1 月 1 日起，馬博策先生被任命為上海諾基亞貝爾執行長暨諾基亞大中華區總裁。
在此之前，馬博策擔任諾基亞歐洲區業務負責人，負責該區域的銷售、行銷、業務管理及運營。

自 2016 年起，馬博策也擔任「數位歐洲 (DIGITALEUROPE)」總裁一職，該組織是歐洲數位技術產業領域最具影響力的產業協會，總部位於布魯塞爾。馬博策亦曾任諾基亞通信大中華區負責人，帶領公司成為中國通信市場首屈一指的外商企業。

他早期任職的職位涵蓋全球銷售、策略、行銷、產品組合管理、業務單位管理及關鍵客戶管理。
馬博策擁有慕尼黑工業大學電機工程碩士學位和麻省理工學院工商管理碩士學位 (MBA)。

With more than 25 years of international experience in the telecommunications, IT and IT Services industries, Markus is a leader who cherishes the opportunity of transformational and turn-around assignments.

Markus was the Head of Europe at Nokia. In this role he led all operations of Nokia in Europe including sales, marketing, business management, operations and delivery. Since 2016, Markus also serves as President of DIGITALEUROPE, a Brussel-based industry association representing the digital technology industry in Europe.

Prior to his current position, Markus served as President of Nokia Greater China. During his time in China, he managed to establish Nokia as the leading non-Chinese supplier in the region.

Earlier leadership positions include roles in global sales, strategy, marketing, portfolio management, business unit management and key account management.

Markus holds a MS in Electrical Engineering from the Technical University of Munich and an MBA from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. In 2014, he was presented with the China Friendship Award.
