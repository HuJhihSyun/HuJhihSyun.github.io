    Kothandaraman, Vimalkumar

    Head of APAC FN BC Adv Presales & Solution

    Vimalkumar Kothandaraman (Vimal) is Head of Fixed Networks Business Centre with-in Network Infrastructure Business Group in APAC region.

    In his current role, Vimal is responsible for Fixed Networks business in APAC region.

    Vimal has held several leadership positions for Fixed Networks as Head of India, Head of Japan and Head of APAC pre-sales, business development & commercial management. During this time, he has worked with many operators in APAC region to help with their Fixed Network strategy.

    Prior to this Vimal has worked in R&D & Product management of Fixed products & solutions.

    With more than 25 years’ experience, Vimal has broad knowledge on Fixed Networks products & solutions and has been enabling new and optimised solutions to overcome challenges for Fixed Network operators in the region.

    He’s currently based out of Melbourne, Australia.

    議程介紹

    Broadband Zero

    Our portfolio strategy including four pillars –

    1. the first pillar is “Zero left behind” , This is all about connecting everyone and everything. Using fiber and fixed wireless solutions to connected every home and every building. Using meshed wifi to connect everyone and everything inside the home. Helping operators design, deploy, and operate networks
    2. the second pillar is Zero limits, Innovative technologies that enbale new use cases and new business cases. Evolving to 25G for business services and 5G transport, app-enabling devices in the home, to support new services and revenu streams. Coming up with innovative approaches to make mmWave affordable
    3. 3rd pillar is Zero touch, This is about automating and optimizing the network. This includes our access and home domain controllers. Our software defined access solutions and cloud integration services
    4. 4th pillar Zero waste, We focus on delivering the benefits of broadband whilst minimizing environmental impact

    我們的解決方案組合策略包含四大支柱：

    1. 第一個支柱是「零遺漏」(Zero left behind)，亦即連結所有的人與物，沒有任何遺漏。利用光纖和固定式無線解決方案連結每個家庭、每棟建築；利用網狀式 Wi-Fi 連接家庭內的所有人與物；協助營運商設計、部署及維運網路。
    2. 第二個支柱是「零限制」(Zero limits)，藉由創新技術實現新的使用案例和商業案例。我們發展支援商業服務的 25G 和 5G 傳輸技術、家庭內使用的應用功能裝置，以引進新的服務和收入來源。推出創新的方法，讓毫米波 (mmWave) 方案變得經濟實惠。
    3. 第三個支柱是零接觸 (Zero touch)，即網路的自動化和優化，這包括我們的接入和家庭網域控制器，軟體定義存取解決方案和雲端整合服務。
    4. 第四個支柱是零廢棄 (Zero waste)，我們致力於提供寬頻的效益，同時儘量減少對環境的影響。
