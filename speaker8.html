REGISTRATION

Giampaolo Panariello

Chief Technical Officer Strategy and Technology, Nokia

Giampaolo Panariello has 30 years of experience in telecommunication. Today in Nokia he is covering the positions of CTO in the Mediterranean Region and CTO for NTN (Non-Terrestrial Networks) Opportunities Development.

He started his career in Alenia Aerospazio (today Tales Alenia Space) as Active Antennas designer. Pioneer of LEO Satellite Constellations, he worked on the Globalstar project as designer of on-board active antennas and in the realization of Small Satellite Integration Centre in Rome for high-rate Globalstar satellite production. Moreover, he was involved in innovative spacecraft projects like Meteosat Second Generation (MSG) and Cosmo Sky-Med. He then moved in Siemens, where he covered the role of Radio Access Team Leader and then Head of Bid Management for 3G Mobile network launches in Italy and Europe.Then in Nokia, where he was Head of Technologies for fixed and mobile networks, responsible for customers in Italy, Mediterranean area and Global, supporting the launch of 4G and 5G Mobile Networks.

Today in Nokia he is CTO for major customers in the Mediterranean region, covering the full portfolio solutions. He is also responsible for Nokia Business/Opportunities Development on Non-Terrestrial Networks He has 30 among participations in conferences, roundtables and publications on specialized magazines.

Giampaolo Panariello 在電信領域已有 30 年的經驗，目前擔任諾基亞地中海區技術長暨 NTN (非地面網路) 商機開發技術長。

他的職業生涯開始於阿萊尼亞航空 (現為泰雷茲阿萊尼亞航太公司)，擔任主動式天線工程師。身為低軌道衛星群的先驅，他曾在 Globalstar 公司專案中擔任機載主動式天線的設計師，並在羅馬建立了小型衛星整合中心，用於生產高速的 Globalstar 衛星。此外，他還參與了創新的太空船專案，如第二代氣象衛星 (MSG) 和 Cosmo Sky-Med。隨後，他服務西門子公司，擔任無線接入部門主管，之後則擔任義大利和歐洲 3G 行動網路部署的投標管理負責人。他在 2000 年加入諾基亞，擔任固定網路和行動網路的技術主管，針對義大利、地中海區和全球的客戶，支援 4G 和 5G 行動網路的部署。

現今他是諾基亞技術長，負責地中海區的主要客戶，並涵蓋所有的組合方案。他還負責諾基亞非地面網路的業務/商機開發。他曾在各種會議、圓桌論壇及專業雜誌上參與分享其專業知識 30 次以上。

Topic Abstract

議程介紹

Non-Terrestrial Networks: An Opportunity for Terrestrial Service Providers?

Low-Earth orbit (LEO) satellites are currently being developed at an unprecedented rate (e.g. Starlink, One-Web, Kuiper, Lightspeed, AST Spacemobile….). These satellite constellations, in particular when aligned with 3GPP specifications, are attracting interest from Terrestrial Operators that wish to deliver broadband connectivity to remote areas due to their low latency and high bandwidth. But, could these Satellite Service Providers grab customers from Terrestrial providers? Not only LEO, but also GEO, MEO Satellite providers are planning NTN-compliant solution. What is the role of High Altitude Platforms (HAPs) in the NTN game? The speech will cover the different aspect of NTNs, including Standardization, Architecture, Security, Sustainability and will discuss opportunities for Terrestrial Service Providers.

當前低軌道 (LEO) 衛星正以前所未有的速度發展 (如 Starlink、One-Web、Kuiper、Lightspeed、AST Spacemobile 等)。這些衛星群，特別是與 3GPP 規範相一致時，由於其低延遲和高頻寬的特性，吸引了希望為偏遠地區提供寬頻連線的地面營運商的興趣。但是，這些衛星服務供應商能從地面營運商搶到客戶嗎？除了 LEO，同步軌道(GEO) 和中軌道 (MEO) 衛星供應商也在規劃符合非地面網路 (NTN) 的解決方案。高空平台 (HAPs) 在 NTN 市場扮演何種角色？此次演講內容將涵蓋 NTN 的不同面向，包括標準化、架構、安全、永續性，並將討論地面營運商的商機。
