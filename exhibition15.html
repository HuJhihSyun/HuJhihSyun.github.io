    Exhibition / 應用展示

    Cloud Big Data Intelligence


    Nokia Deepfield Cloud Intelligence provides service providers (cable providers, cloud providers and Telcos) as well as large enterprises with context-aware content engineering.

    The Cloud Intelligence performs peer analysis and peer prospecting, enabling you to optimize existing peers, eliminate unnecessary network traffic, and thereby reduce paid transit and expensive peer traffic. And because you see the origin of all traffic, you can accurately plan for traffic at peak times.

    With Cloud Intelligence, you can easily optimize today’s networks and manage relationships with content, peering and OTT partners/cloud applications and services.

    諾基亞 Deepfield 雲智慧分析平台為服務提供商（有線電視提供商，雲提供商和電信公司）以及大型企業提供了訊務內容控管工程。雲智慧分析平台能針對網路對等互連端, 執行分析與內容類型探測，使您能夠優化既有網路對等互連點，消除不必要的網絡流量，從而減少付費過境和昂貴的對等流量。 而且由於您可以看到所有流量的來源，甚至您可以在高峰時段精準規劃流量。

    借助 Deepfield 雲智慧分析平台，您可以輕鬆優化現今既有網路，並針對不同的維度條件，例如 流量類型，對等互連端，OTT 承載網路和應用程式類型， 執行更有效與節降成本規劃管理。
