    Exhibition / 應用展示

    Fixed Mobile Convergence Gateway for Mobile Core

    Demonstrate Nokia 7750 SR platform to support both mobile and fixed broadband service into a unify subscriber management control gateway and provide hybrid CPE gateway single IP address capability to support mobile and fixed convergence service with high resilience capability and is the foundation of future 5G FWA GW technology in Core domain.

    展示諾基亞 7750 SR 平台同時支援行動 Mobile 與固網 BNG/BRAS 技術，以單一平台 7750 平台提供終端用戶裝置 (CPE Hybrid Gateway) 以相同用戶 IP address 同時使用行動網路與固定網路以提供高可靠度異質網路用戶服務體驗並且可供享行動與固網接取網路頻寬，提供未來 5G 網路 FWA 技術核心網路基礎架構。
