Exhibition / 應用展示

Precise Indoor Positioning

室內精準定位的需求在眾多 5G 應用場景已日漸增加，這些需求推升業者在數位轉型過程中的操作需要定位精準至小於 1m，工業 4.0 中的無人搬運車、自動機器人、倉儲、機場的工具與人員定位、醫院設備找尋等等都需要精準定位。

    Nokia 5G 室內精準定位方案提供了比現有室內定位技術更好的效益:
  • 可靠度提升，更精準的定位，可用小於 1m 的精確範圍
  • 無須額外標籤，定位系統與接收裝置可用同一網路連接，如 5G 網路
  • 降低建置成本與網路複雜度
本方案利用 ULTDoA 即 AoA 兩種方式結合可將準確率提升至 93 % - 95 % (~50 cm) 並於 2020 年 十二月由 Nokia Bell Lab 驗證成功於工業 4.0 應用 (ARENA 2036)。

Factories, warehouses, ports and airports are digitalizing their and need to enable use cases that rely on both reliable data and precise positioning (10-50cm) indoor/areas.

The need for 5G precise positioning especially for indoor case such as AMR/AGV in industrial 4.0 and asset finding, worker, collaborative mobile, critical tool etc. are arising even without pushing.

    Nokia 5G precise positioning provide the following benefits than existing technology being used such as BLE, UWB
  • More reliability
  • No need for an extra tag
  • Reduced complexity and lower TCO
By using the combined ULTDoA and AoA methodology, the positioning accuracy can achieve up to > 90% in sub meter level (~50 cm) which is defined based on Bell PoC with BOSCH / Omron factory.

Full 5G locator using the 2 methods above is being built and will be tested by Bell Labs in ARENA 2036 (industry 4.0 hall) between July-December 20.
