REGISTRATION

Harri Holma

Technology Leadership Officer Advisor, Nokia Bell Labs

Harri Holma joined Nokia Research Center in 1994 and received his M.Sc. from Helsinki University of Technology 1995. He has been with Nokia since 1994 and has been located both in Finland and in USA during that time. Harri Holma is currently working as Fellow and Advisor in Technology Office in Nokia with special interest on radio technologies and mobile networks. He has completed his PhD at Helsinki University of Technology 2003. Dr. Holma has edited the books "WCDMA for UMTS", "HSDPA/HSUPA for UMTS", "LTE for UMTS", “Voice over LTE”, “LTE Advanced”, “HSPA+ Evolution”, “LTE Small Cell Optimization” and “5G Technology”, and contributed to a number of other books in the radio communication area.

Harri Holma 於 1994 年加入諾基亞研究中心，並於 1995 年取得赫爾辛基科技大學理學碩士學位。1994 年加入諾基亞後，他曾在芬蘭和美國分公司工作。

Harri Holma 目前擔任諾基亞技術辦公室的研究員 (Fellow) 和顧問，特別關注無線電技術和行動網路。他於 2003 年在赫爾辛基科技大學完成博士學位。Holma 博士編輯了《WCDMA for UMTS》、《HSDPA/HSUPA for UMTS》、《LTE for UMTS》、《Voice over LTE》、《LTE Advanced》、《HSPA+ Evolution》、《LTE Small Cell Optimization》以及《5G Technology》等著作，並參與其他無線電通信書籍的寫作。

Topic Abstract

議程介紹

5G learnings and forward to 5G-Advanced

Harri will walk us through the current 5G network trend primarily around the network performance and growth. We’ll reveal the development of some 5G advance countries that we could take reference for to identify the possible technology trend going forward to meet the growth and application requirements. 5G-Advanced is being developed in 3GPP and target to address more advance 5G use cases and enterprise needs. Harri will provide insights into the timeline of 5G-Advanced and main focused areas along with key technology details. Key elements to 6G transition will be discussed as the finale of the speech.
