REGISTRATION

Leslie Shannon

Head of Ecosystem and Trend Scouting, Nokia

Leslie Shannon is the Head of Ecosystem and Trend Scouting for Nokia. A Silicon Valley-based futurist, she focuses on identifying tech disruptions and opportunities for Nokia and its customers, including 5G-powered developments in robotics, drones, visual analytics, cloud gaming, and especially Augmented and Virtual Reality, the foundations of the Metaverse. Leslie has a BA from the University of Virginia and a Master’s Degree from Yale University. She does all her daily fitness work in Virtual Reality.

Leslie Shannon 為諾基亞生態系統與趨勢探索總監。身為矽谷的未來學家，她致力於為諾基亞及其客戶找出顛覆性創新的技術和機會，其領域涵蓋了由 5G 驅動的機器人、無人機、視覺分析和雲端遊戲的發展，特別是元宇宙的技術基礎：擴增實境 (AR) 和虛擬實境 (VR)。

Leslie Shannon 擁有維吉尼亞大學學士學位和耶魯大學碩士學位。她日常的健身活動都是在虛擬實境中進行的。

Topic Abstract

議程介紹

5G: Enterprise Today, Metaverse Tomorrow

In this use-case focused presentation, Leslie Shannon will examine the global instances in which 5G is being used to do things that can’t be done by 4G or Wi-Fi. The range of these use cases will cover today’s Enterprise applications, and look forward to the early foundations of what will grow to be a significant part of the Consumer Metaverse over the next several years. The future is closer than we think!

在這場以使用案例為重點的演講中，Leslie Shannon 將分享全球用戶利用 5G 來實現 4G 或 Wi-Fi 所無法達成的任務。這些使用案例涵蓋現今的企業應用，並前瞻未來幾年即將發展成為消費者元宇宙關鍵要件的初期技術基礎。未來出現之速，超乎我們的想像。
