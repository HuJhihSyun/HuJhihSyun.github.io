    REGISTRATION

    Jason Liu

    Vice President, Greater China Market, Nokia
    President of Nokia Solutions and Networks Taiwan

    Jason has been in Nokia Taiwan since the year of 2000. From 2009 to 2018 he served as Head of Taiwan Mobile Customer Team, Great China, Customer Operations. Prior to this, he served as a senior account manager and system marketing manager for Nokia Taiwan. He has built very strong foundation in both internally and externally which indeed has helped to strengthen Nokia’s position in Taiwan market. On August 1, 2018, Jason assumed the role as President of Nokia Solutions and Networks Taiwan. He was in charge of major business development and operational improvement in the Taiwan market.

    Previously, he has served as senior manager of the Wireless Account Team - Technical Management of Lucent Taiwan. Prior to that, Jason was a wireless system RF Section Manager in the KG Telecom Project. Jason’s earlier experience includes 1 year of Satellite assistant researcher in Nation Space Organization (NSPO) and 2 year of RF engineer for CATV business in AT&T TT.

    Jason is a graduate of the National Taiwan University Executive Master of Business Administration and holds a Master Degree in Telecommunications from the National Chaio-Tung University.

    劉明達自 2000 年起加入台灣諾基亞，在 2009 年至 2018 年期間擔任大中華區客戶營運部門主管，負責台灣大哥大客戶服務。在此之前，曾擔任資深專案經理和系統營運銷售經理，以深厚的通信技術專業為基礎，深獲客戶信賴，協助諾基亞於台灣市場奠定堅實的基礎。自2018 年 8 月 1 日接任台灣諾基亞總經理一職，負責主導業務開發、營運管理。

    劉明達在電信相關產業擁有豐富的職場歷練，曾擔任朗訊科技有限公司台灣區無線用戶團隊與技術管理部門資深經理，負責和信電訊無線網路規劃，也曾擔任台灣國家實驗研究院國家太空中心 (NSPO) 衛星助理研究員，以及美台電訊公司 (AT＆T-TT) 有線電視業務 RF 工程師。
