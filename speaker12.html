    REGISTRATION

    Michael Chang

    Chief Technology Officer, Customer Operations, Greater China Market, Nokia

    Michael Chang is the Chief Technology Officer, Customer Operations for Nokia Greater China. Before the CTO role, Michael has been heading Industry Environment APAC of Bell-Labs, responsible for Nokia standardization and technical regulation in APAC region. He has over 17 years of managerial experience in multinational companies on ICT research & innovation, standardization and technology strategy.

    Starting with Lucent Technologies Bell-Labs as a senior researcher, Michael developed his professional career from defining key technologies for 3G WCDMA/TD-SCDMA, where he held 12 patents in US and China. Joining Nokia 13 years ago, he assumed the role to lead tech vision & strategy, standardization and technical policies, where he has made extensive contribution to drive the global mobile standard harmonization with China on major milestones. His focus is now on 5G, IoT, Cloud Computing, Open Source, Cybersecurity and the emerging tech trends e.g. ML/AI.

    Elected as Vice President and Board Member of EUCCC (EU Chamber of Commerce in China) in May 2017, Michael has been leading the high-tech policy dialogue between EU and China representing EU industry. He also serves as a member of the “EU-China Expert Group on Digital Economy & Cybersecurity” formed by China & EU authorities as the top dialogue platform on digital transformation.

    常疆為諾基亞大中華區市場客戶營運部門技術長。常疆在擔任技術長一職前，一直帶領著貝爾實驗室亞太區工業環境部門，負責亞太區的諾基亞標準化與技術規範。他在跨國公司的 ICT 研究與創新、標準化與技術策略方面擁有超過 17 年的管理經驗。

    常疆最早在朗訊科技貝爾實驗室擔任資深研究員，從訂定 3G WCDMA／TD-SCDMA 關鍵技術展開他的工作生涯，在美國和中國擁有 12 項專利。在加入諾基亞的 13 年前，他擔任帶領技術願景和策略、標準化及技術政策的職務，對於推動與中國合作在全球行動標準協調方面達到重要里程碑，有著重大貢獻。他目前的重點為 5G、物聯網、雲端運算、開放源碼、網路安全以及 ML／AI 等新興技術趨勢。

    2017年5月獲選為 EUCCC（中國歐盟商會）副會長兼董事會成員的常疆，一直代表歐盟產業在歐盟與中國之間帶領進行高科技政策對話。他還擔任由中國與歐盟當局成立之「EU-China Expert Group on Digital Economy & Cybersecurity」（歐盟–中國數位經濟與網路安全專家小組）的成員，作為數位轉型的首要對話平台。
