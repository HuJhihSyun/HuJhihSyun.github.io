    議程介紹

    5G Security

    5G supports massive amount of devices to be connected to the network. 5G will also enable the huge of large amounts of bandwidth. This creates an entirely different threat landscape. In case of 5G security challenges emanate from the very improvements that 5G will bring in.

    Several institutions raised concerns for 5G with respect to Security and called for a holistic security approach to protect 5G eco system. Internet of Things is one of the major initiatives that will receive its fillip due to 5G and this is one major avenue for attack, since 5G enables IoT to be much larger than on previous networks. The future of communications will drive new 5G security requirements, because of concerns for new use cases and new network architectures. New cloud/virtualization technologies such as software-defined networking (SDN) and network functions virtualization (NFV) are thriving in anticipation of 5G networks, but they too come with new security concerns. Because of their open, flexible, programmable nature, SDN and NFV open up a new avenue of security threats. For example, a network element of an SDN such as the management interfaces could be used to attack the SDN controller or management system and bring down the system.

    The presentation covers the trends in 5G security, best practices that need to be adopted and provides a blue print for a holistic 5G security approach.

    5G 可支援大規模的裝置連上網路，也能提供超大規模的頻寬，這些特性造成了完全不同於以往的安全威脅。5G 為我們所帶來的便利進步，同時也讓環境面臨不同的安全挑戰。

    數個研究機構都對 5G 提出了安全方面的憂慮，並呼籲採用整體性的途徑來保護 5G 生態系統。物聯網 (IoT) 將是因 5G 而興起的重要方案之一，但相較於過去的網路，5G 會讓物聯網的規模大上許多，使得物聯網成為主要的安全攻擊途徑之一。由於新的使用案例和網路架構帶來了安全上的疑慮，未來的通訊服務將驅動新的 5G 安全要求。基於 5G 網路興起而產生的許多期望，還包括新的雲端/虛擬化技術，如軟體定義網路 (SDN) 和網路功能虛擬化 (NFV)，但這些新技術也伴隨新的安全問題，這是因為 SDN 和 NFV 都具備開放、彈性、可程式化的特性，開啟了新的安全威脅途徑。例如 SDN 的網路元件之一「管理介面」，可被用來攻擊 SDN 控制器或管理系統，進而擊垮系統。

    本議程將分享關於 5G 安全議題的趨勢、最佳實務案例、以及整體性 5G 安全規劃藍圖。
