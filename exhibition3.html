Exhibition / 應用展示

5G Core’s New Horizons

諾基亞明確的推動 5G 網路的演進，包括 T-Mobile USA 於 2020 年 8 月在全國範圍內推出 5G 獨立組網，以及 DISH Wireless USA 準備在公共雲上推出世界第一的 5G 服務。 探索具有高頻寬和低延遲特性的網路設計，超大規模的公有雲/混合雲部署，以及高可靠性和可擴充性的信令處理網路。

通過 DevOps CI / CD、自動化和網路切片以符合業務需求及運營策略，或者通過 API 創新探索新業務和開發新的服務類型，例如工業採礦和AR購物模式，探索如何通過商業價值和差異化的案例，從而產生新的收入和機會。

Nokia brings clarity to Network evolution paths into 5G, including world firsts at T-Mobile USA with its nationwide launch of 5G standalone in August 2020, and DISH Wireless USA’s readiness to launch 5G on public cloud. Explore network design with best-in-class bandwidth & latency, deployment onto hyperscale’s’ public/hybrid clouds, and signaling for reliability & scale.

See the Operations strategy that best fits your business demands with DevOps CI/CD, automation, and slicing. Or explore new business with API innovation and demand new services such as industrial mining and augmented-reality enhanced shopping. Explore how to monetize use-cases with business values and differentiators that will generate new revenues and opportunities.
