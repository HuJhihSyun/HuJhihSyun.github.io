REGISTRATION

Susanna Patja

Head of Cloud & Network Services Business Group, Greater China Market, Nokia

Susanna Patja has over 20 experience in the telecommunications industry and is currently leading Nokia’s Cloud and Network Services (CNS) Business Group in the Greater China Region.

Susanna is responsible for all CNS sales, technical presales, delivery, R&D and marketing activities towards CSPs, webscales and enterprises. Having spent her teenage years in Beijing in the 1980’s, she is particularly passionate about the 5G and Industry 4.0. opportunities and speed of development in the Greater China Region.

Susanna Patja 在電信業有超過 20 年的經驗，目前領導諾基亞大中華區的雲和網路服務 （CNS) 事業群。

Susanna 負責所有電信業者、網路業者和企業的 CNS 銷售、售前規劃、交付、研發和行銷活動。

1980 年代時，她在北京度過青少年時期，因此她尤其熱衷於 5G 和工業 4.0 在大中華區的發展機會和速度。
