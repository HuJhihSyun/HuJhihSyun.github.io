Exhibition / 應用展示

Optics Evolution & TDM Modernization

Innovative PSE-3s chip taking light to the limit
The PSE-3s DSP chip, using Probabilistic Constellation Shaping represents the best of Nokia’s latest coherent optical technology. Where another DSP would leave some margin on the table with 16-QAM at 200G, but 32-QAM would fail, the PSE-3s can continuously adjust to a bandwidth that sits logically in-between these two points. This process effectively shaves off the last bit of linear noise margin to close the gap with the Shannon Limit, the maximum theoretical capacity of the channel.

Network modernization with PDH/SDH mapping into OTN
Service providers need to meet the growing and dynamic traffic demands of high-bandwidth, cloud-based services but must also continue to support TDM services. Nokia turnkey TDM renewal solution reduces both OpEx and CapEx, meanwhile it also helps accelerate revenue generation by selectively migrating costly legacy network domains to a modern OTN/ WDM transport infrastructure. As a result, network resources are freed up to support profitable revenue streams.

創新的 PSE-3s 晶片將光傳輸更推向極限
PSE-3s 晶片使用 PCS (Probabilistic Constellation Shaping) 調變技術，同時也是 Nokia 最新最佳的同調光傳輸技術。當其他晶片業者因無法實現 32-QAM 調變技術而停留在 16-QAM 200Gbps 卻餘留部分可貴 optical power margin 時，PSE-3s 可以持續自我調節頻寬在 16-QAM 200G 與 32-QAM 之間。這個程序有效地充分利用剩餘 optical power margin，拉近與 Shannon Limit 所謂的理論最大傳輸速率之間的距離。

網路現代化解決方案 PDH/SDH 直接映射至 OTN
運營商眼下的挑戰，網路需要能夠乘載持續成長而且多元的訊務要求，例如：高頻寬、雲化服務，但同時也需要繼續提供既有的 TDM 服務。Nokia 一站式的 TDM 更新解決方案，可以降低運營商 OpEx 與 CapEx，同時又能加速新服務收入的提供，關鍵在於可以選擇性地升級維護上最昂貴的網路區域成為現代化的 OTN/WDM 傳輸網路架構。透過此一升級過程而釋放出來的網路資源，就能用於更高收益的服務使用。
